Rhys J. Evans

rhysjohn.evans@sydney.edu.au

G’day! I am a Magma research fellow working in the Comptutational algebra group at the University of Sydney, School of Mathematics and Statistics

My research interests include algebraic graph theory, combinatorics, finite geometry, and related computational aspects.

Previously, I was based in the Inštitut za matematiko, fiziko in mehaniko in Ljubljana, Slovenia, where I worked on creating and curating datasets of highly symmetrical discrete objects with Primož Potočnik. I also worked at Sobolev Institute of Mathematics, in the Applied Abstract Algebra group led by Alexander Mednykh. I received my PhD in Mathematics at Queen Mary University of London in 2020, under the supervision of Professor Leonard H. Soicher.
