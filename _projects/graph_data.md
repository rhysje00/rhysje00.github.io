Working in algebraic graph theory, I often find myself using and contributing to several datasets of graphs. On this page, I list these datasets and give methods to access the data directly or indirectly.

<br>

## Self-promotion
---

In the table below I list the datasets of graphs I have currently been involved in creating and/or curating.

<br>
<div class="datatable-begin"></div>

Name             | Description                                                                    | Access           |
--------------------   | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------   | --------------------------   |
Cubic Cayley graphs       | Complete for orders 4-5000 | [4-5000](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15775470)   |
Quartic Cayley graphs | Complete for orders 5-1025                                              | [5-1025](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15775678) |
Minimal Cayley graphs      | Complete for orders 2-511    | [2-511](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15704803), [384](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15721425)  |
GraphSym       | Several classes of highly symmetric graphs, and more | [link](https://graphsym.net/)   |
Neumaier graphs     | Partial enumeration and sporadic generation     | [link](/projects/neumaier_graphs/#small-strictly-neumaier-graphs) |

<div class="datatable-end"></div>

## Other collections
---

In the table below is a list of other sources of graphs and related data that are useful to have at hand. 

This list does not contain some well-known collections of graphs found online, but many of these be found through the House of graphs' [meta-directory](https://houseofgraphs.org/meta-directory).

<br>
<div class="datatable-begin"></div>

Name             | Description                                                                    | Access           |
--------------------   | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------   | --------------------------   |
House of Graphs      | A database of small graphs | [link](https://houseofgraphs.org/), [meta](https://houseofgraphs.org/meta-directory)   |
PHOEG web UI      | A web UI to search for small graphs | [link](https://phoeg.umons.ac.be/phoeg/)   |
Vertex-transitive graphs    | Complete for orders 10-47 | [link](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4010122)   |
